Yes, LinkedIn is one of the best "hubs" — but not the only one, and not sufficient if it stands alone.

If the target is Mbah's Innovators & Early Adopters, they typically don't gather in one place — they're distributed across several "ecosystem layers." Ya, LinkedIn itu salah satu "hub" terbaik — tapi bukan satu-satunya, dan tidak cukup kalau berdiri sendiri.
