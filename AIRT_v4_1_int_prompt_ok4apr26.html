[A1] Novelty Spectrum — Rubrik Operasional
[A1] Novelty Spectrum — Operational Rubric
Level LabelLabel Indikator Konkret (Reproducible)Concrete Indicator (Reproducible)
1 ReproduksiReproduction Kalimat/paragraf identik atau hampir identik dengan sumber yang diketahui. Tidak acceptable tanpa atribusi penuh. Sentences/paragraphs identical or near-identical to known sources. Not acceptable without full attribution.
2 ParafraseParaphrase Ide sama, kata berbeda. Struktur argumen mengikuti sumber asli. Acceptable hanya dengan atribusi eksplisit. Same idea, different words. Argument structure follows the original source. Acceptable only with explicit attribution.
3 SintesisSynthesis Menggabungkan ≥2 konsep yang sudah ada menjadi satu kerangka baru. Sumber masing-masing wajib diidentifikasi. Combining ≥2 existing concepts into a new framework. Each source must be identified.
4 ReinterpretasiReinterpretation Sudut pandang baru atas konsep existing. Penulis memberi kontribusi interpretif yang berbeda dari sumber asli. New perspective on an existing concept. The author provides an interpretive contribution different from the original source.
5 Kontribusi OrisinalOriginal Contribution Konsep, terminologi, atau framework tidak ditemukan dalam training data Lingua maupun literatur yang diketahui. Concept, terminology, or framework not found in Lingua's training data or known literature.
Peta kontribusi konten: tandai proporsi EXISTING / DEVELOPED / CO-CREATED / SYNTHESIZED dalam estimasi persentase per dokumen.
Content contribution map: mark the proportion of EXISTING / DEVELOPED / CO-CREATED / SYNTHESIZED in estimated percentages per document.

CoT v4.1 format is specifically designed for plagiarism auditing, not a generic format. Every critical assessment must pass through these 5 steps.
Step Pertanyaan yang dijawab LinguaQuestion answered by Lingua
